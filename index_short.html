Why Choose Me

I tailor my guidance to address the unique challenges and needs of each family. Here are some key aspects:

  1. Interactive Workshops: Hands-on sessions that encourage active participation.
  2. Evidence-Based Strategies: Reliable tools backed by research.
  3. Role-Playing and Simulations: Practice scenarios for real-life situations.
  4. Collaborative Learning: Peer learning enhances understanding and empathy.
  5. Continuous Support and Feedback: Regular check-ins for tracking progress.
  6. Flexible Programs: Online/offline options tailored for individual or group needs.

Parenting Coaching

Parent coaching involves a strengths-based approach to improving challenging behaviors in children. It provides strategies to help parents become the role models they aspire to be.

  1. Sort Yourself Out: Helps parents become their ideal selves.
  2. Help Children Cope: Manage social and academic pressures effectively.
  3. See Through Children’s Eyes: Broaden perspectives for better understanding.
  4. Improve Connection: Build effective communication skills.
  5. Address Behavior: Tackle unwanted traits with suitable strategies.
